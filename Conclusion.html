Conclusion



In conclusion, this literature portfolio is the symbol of my completion of this wonderful subject.
The journey of this subject taught me many things, I learned that literature is not just a simple story making and it has a deeper voyage in it.
This also widened my horizon by unlocking my inner writer and coming up with a story is really fun and creative.
Through the analysis and interpretation of these texts, a deeper understanding of the power of language and storytelling has emerged.
Each piece contributes to the mosaic of literature, reminding us of its ability to transcend time and culture, resonating with readers across generations.
Generations that greatly honed and molded the concept of literature across the different timelines; wars, peace, celebrations, sorrows, and independence.
This taught me that literature is not just a story, it is an art, a masterpiece, a form of expression where every individual has the freedom to exhibit their creative imagination.

Expression of an individual affected by experience.
As I close this portfolio, I carry with me a renewed appreciation for the art of words and the unique ways in which literature captures the essence of our shared humanity.
